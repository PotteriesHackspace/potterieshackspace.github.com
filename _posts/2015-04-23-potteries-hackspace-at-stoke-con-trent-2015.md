Hopefully you saw us at Stoke Con Trent 2015 and you&#8217;re eager to come and visit us at one of our open nights, every Tuesday from 7PM onwards.

The Comic Con was a great success. Our cycle generator and Raspberry Pi powered display really attracted a lot of genuine interest.

<!--more-->

Thanks to Matt and the team for their hard work and support.

To celebrate we held an a little party on Tuesday with a BBQ and free beer at the Hackspace.
